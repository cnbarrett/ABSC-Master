ABSC

Albany-Berkeley Soccer Club (ABSC) is a non-profit soccer community. We have 44+ years dedicated to providing opportunities for kids ages 4 through 23 of all ability levels to learn and play soccer. We encourage our players to live a life-long healthy active lifestyle though soccer.
